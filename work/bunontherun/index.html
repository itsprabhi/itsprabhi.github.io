Bun on the Run

Responsibilities - web design, UI design

About the project

Bun on the Run is a service offered by Georgian college to its students. The purpose of this service is to provide high-quality and affordable food to students in a rush. The food is cooked by students learning the art of culinary. Thus, it helps students in many different ways. This project was part of a college project where we had to develop a campaign to promote Bun on the Run and let students know about this outstanding service.

Creative process

The purpose of designing this landing page is brand awareness. The design must include all the information about the service and make students get food from there. The colors we chose were yellow because it stands out and makes it look more friendly. The call-to-actions for this site are to view the menu and learn more about the service.

There are three sections of the landing page. The first section explains the service and why students should go there. Another shows reviews from students to establish credibility. The landing page also includes a section to tell about all the payment methods to make it easy for students to buy. Overall the layout is simple, intuitive, and easy to read.
